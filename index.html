About paycrunch.co

We build free, fast financial calculators that give you instant answers without requiring signup, email, or personal information. Every calculation runs locally in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

Our tax data comes from IRS.gov, salary data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS.gov), and cost-of-living metrics from government surveys. We update our data annually to ensure accuracy.

Disclaimer: All calculations are estimates for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial, tax, legal, or accounting advice. Federal tax uses 2024 IRS brackets with standard deduction. State rates are approximate. Consult a qualified professional before making financial decisions.
